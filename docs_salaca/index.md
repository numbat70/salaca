		- Mazsalaca 15km into the race
		- Lagaste 80km in at the start of the rapids
		- Salacagriva 100km at the Finish

### Tides
- There are no meaningful tides in the BALTIC
  BUT
  Heavy wind can drive water into the river mouths and make them feel slightly tidal. 
  
  Check the wind forecast for strong westerly's.
